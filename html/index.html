Bumbung 12 Gallery

The exhibition displays some of the photographs depicting the cordial relationship between Brunei and the United Kingdom since late 19th century until present time. The photographs also show how Brunei developed during the resident era and the many changes since independence in 1984. Bumbung Dua Belas is divided into five galleries.

Industrial Site

The Labu Estate Rubber Industrial Site in the Temburong district has potential to become one of Brunei’s tourist attractions and complement its role as an educational site for students and researchers. The Labu Estate Rubber Industry Site was launched on August 5, 2010. An ‘open site museum’ concept, has significant historical, economic and cultural values.
